Come on in

A little local,
a lot of heart.

We set up the cafe the way we wished every corner feed joint was: honest menu, warm welcome, a counter you can chat over, and a kitchen where you can see Rob working the grill. No TV playing the news. No stiff hostess stand. Just a place to eat a good burger with people you like.

Pop in for a feed. Stay for a yarn. If it's your first time, tell us — first-timers gets a story from the family.

Good to know

Quick Questions

Do you take bookings?

For groups under 10, just pop in. For 10 or more, give us a call a day or two ahead so we can save you the back deck.

When's the full menu available?

Lunch menu kicks off at 11am every day we're open. Brekkie runs Sat & Sun 8–11am. After 11am on weekends the full menu's on.

Dietary options?

GF buns and dairy-free cheese on request ($2 swap). Vegetarian options available — chat to Rob if you've got allergies, he'll sort you.

Where are you, exactly?

448 Pacific Highway, Crows Nest NSW 2065. Look for the pink neon pig in the window — you'll know you're in the right spot.

See you at the Cafe.

No fuss, no reservations (unless you're rolling deep), no attitude. Just a good feed and a good yarn.
