Important Information

A few quick things worth knowing before you book.

Minimum 50 Guests

Our minimum booking requirement is 50 people. On bigger jobs we offer an alternating menu so everyone gets something they love.

Burgers Include Fries

Every burger on the menu comes with a side of fries, included in the per-guest price. No need to add extra.

Serving Times

The more deep-fried items you choose, the longer serving times may be (the oil cools down with frequent use). For a balanced, efficient service, we recommend selecting one beef burger and one chicken burger.

Alternating Menus

On bigger jobs we serve an alternating menu — half the guests get one burger, the other half a different one. Makes service quicker and gives everyone a peek at what their mate got.

Pricing & Travel

All prices are plus GST. A travel fee may apply depending on your location — we'll quote the lot up front so there are no surprises.

Crowd Favourites

Our most popular selections are the pulled beef brisket, pulled pork and the sticky chicken burgers. Can't go wrong with any of them.

Allergies & Dietary

Please let us know about any food allergies or dietary requirements when you book so we can plan accordingly.

Vegetarian, GF & Vegan

We've got vegetarian, gluten-free and vegan options available. These need to be discussed directly with Rob so we can sort the right setup for your event.

Quick answers

FAQ

What's the minimum headcount?

50 people. For smaller groups we can point you towards a few options or take bookings at the Porkies cafe instead.

How far will you travel?

We'll go a fair way for the right gig. Travel fees kick in outside our usual area — we'll be straight with you about cost up front.

Do you cater dietary requirements?

Yes — vegetarian, gluten-free, and vegan options are all available, but need to be discussed with Rob directly on 0414 739 696 so we can plan the right setup. All allergies and dietary requirements must be advised when you book.

What does an "alternating menu" mean?

On bigger jobs we serve an alternating menu — half the guests get one burger, the other half a different one. Speeds up service and gives everyone variety.

Are prices per head final?

Per-guest prices are plus GST. A travel fee may apply depending on location — we'll quote the full amount up front so there are no surprises.

How do I lock in a date?

Ring Rob on 0414 739 696 or DM @porkiesmobilecatering on Instagram. We'll chat through the menu, confirm availability, and lock it in with a small deposit.

How long does set-up take?

Rob rolls up about an hour before service starts to set up and fire the grills. Service itself is quick once we're rolling — especially if you follow our tip of picking one beef and one chicken burger.
